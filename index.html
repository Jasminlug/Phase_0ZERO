01
Objective

Phase 0 is not a full-scale launch. It is a rapid Proof of Concept with one measurable outcome: acquiring 10 to 20 patients who commit to a hair protocol subscription and provide payment details.

Backend automation, formal MSO legal infrastructure, and AI tracking belong to Phase 1. However, Brand Identity begins immediately in Phase 0. While the backend runs manually, the patient-facing touchpoints must look premium from day one. A strong brand identity also elevates our enterprise valuation ahead of any future investor discussions.

What Phase 0 proves: That the referral flow works. That the offer converts. That patients are willing to subscribe for this specific clinical wrapper.

02
Clinical Framework: The Peretz Medical Protocol Pyramid

The architecture below reflects Dr. Eric and Dr. Assi Peretz’s clinical philosophy, defining the full spectrum of hair restoration intervention from foundational treatment to elective enhancements.

The Peretz Medical Protocol Pyramid — from Foundation & Lifestyle to Advanced Experimental

The PROOF Lane: The pyramid defines the full clinical spectrum. Finasteride/Dutasteride and Minoxidil form the clinical base. The Healthy Lifestyle axis supports everything above it — nutrition, sleep, stress management, and targeted supplementation create the environment in which the protocol delivers results. PROOF’s subscription operates at its foundation and delivers both.

03
Phase 0 Patient Source

Rather than guessing with cold paid campaigns, Phase 0 strategically leverages one of our strongest assets: the trust already established by Peretz Medical. Since patients receive these prescriptions as a baseline treatment anyway, the clinical recommendation from the physicians is incredibly powerful.

It is important that the physicians are never positioned as salespeople. Peretz Medical provides the clinical recommendation; the commercial conversion is handled entirely by PROOF. This preserves the physician’s medical authority and keeps the patient relationship clean.

Patient Flow

Peretz Medical consults with patients daily across their clinics. Per their clinical framework, the foundation protocol is recommended to the majority of consulting patients as standard of care.

The Handoff

The flow from clinical recommendation to PROOF onboarding will be mapped collaboratively with Peretz Medical, ensuring the transition feels natural for both physician and patient.

05
Commercial and Regulatory Structure

The subscription model is structured so that prescription fulfilment flows through a licensed pharmacy partner, and PROOF manages the clinical subscription and platform wrapper. This structure ensures full regulatory compliance under Israeli MOH requirements.

01
Compliance Flow
Or has secured a green light from a partner pharmacy to run this pilot. We will execute either a manual split-payment or a consolidated settlement, reconciled between the pharmacy and OG Capital. OG Capital is consulting with a specialist pharmaceutical regulatory lawyer to finalise the exact legal mechanism for Phase 0.
02
Manual Execution
All Phase 0 billings and renewals will be handled manually. Or will personally handle all Phase 0 patient conversions and interactions to learn patient objections firsthand, optimise the flow, and ensure a premium concierge experience before building the automation.
03
Data Protection
Patient data collection in Phase 0 is subject to the Israeli Protection of Privacy Law 5741-1981. Formal database registration is a Phase 1 requirement. Phase 0 operates under explicit WhatsApp consent collected from each patient at the start of intake.
